Guided Process

Four stages, each designed to reduce friction.

This is not a thin booking flow. Sana Academy combines fit guidance, a structured trial, premium teacher coordination, and visible progress support so the process feels dependable from the start.

01

Browse & Discover

Clarify the right starting point before you commit.

Explore teachers or structured programs, compare teaching styles, and share your goals. If you need direction, an advisor helps narrow the best fit by level, schedule, and learning outcome.

You share

Subject, current level, preferred times, and what success should look like.

Sana handles

Fit guidance, shortlist logic, and cleaner comparison of your best options.

During the first lesson, your teacher checks readiness, teaching chemistry, and target outcomes. If the fit is strong, Sana helps define cadence, format, and the next milestone so learning starts with structure.

Diagnosis, live teaching sample, and a realistic view of pacing and expectations.
