Biography

About Leila

97% rebook after trial

Leila teaches ambitious learners who want more than casual language exposure. Her sessions are built around structured speaking practice, vocabulary precision, and visible progress week by week. She works with students preparing for relocation, university pathways, diplomatic work, and bilingual family life.

Before joining Sana Academy, she taught Arabic and academic English in university language centers and private executive programs. That background shows in the way she builds lessons: clear outcomes, intelligent correction, and just enough pressure to keep momentum high without overwhelming the learner.

Parents and adult learners choose her when they need credibility, consistency, and honest feedback. Her profile attracts students who want a teacher capable of handling pronunciation, writing, fluency, and test readiness with equal seriousness.
