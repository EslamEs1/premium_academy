Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Sana Academy collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you browse the website, contact the academy, or engage with our services.

Last updated

March 15, 2026

This policy applies to the Sana Academy public website and academy inquiries unless a separate written agreement states otherwise.

Sections

1. Information Collection

Sana Academy may collect information you voluntarily provide through contact forms, inquiry fields, newsletter forms, or other direct communication channels. This may include your name, email address, phone number, subject of interest, learner level, schedule preferences, and message details.

2. How We Use Information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, recommend teachers or programs, support scheduling, improve service quality, and maintain internal records related to academy operations. We may also use limited information to understand how visitors engage with the website and improve its clarity and usefulness.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The website may use cookies or similar technologies to support functionality, preserve preferences, understand user interactions, and improve browsing performance. Cookies do not typically identify you directly, but they may contribute to how the site recognizes repeat visits or user behavior patterns.

4. Third-Party Services

Sana Academy may rely on third-party providers for infrastructure, analytics, communication, or operational support. Where third-party services process information on our behalf, we expect them to maintain appropriate confidentiality and data protection standards.

5. Data Security

We take commercially reasonable steps to protect information from unauthorized access, misuse, disclosure, or alteration. No method of transmission or storage is guaranteed to be fully secure, but Sana Academy aims to maintain practical safeguards appropriate to the type of information collected.

6. Children's Privacy

Sana Academy may work with minors through parent or guardian involvement. We expect inquiries relating to minors to be submitted by or with the knowledge of a responsible parent or guardian. If we learn that personal information has been submitted improperly by a child without appropriate supervision, we may remove or limit related records.

7. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights relating to access, correction, deletion, objection, or restriction of certain personal information. If you wish to exercise a privacy-related request, you may contact the academy using the details below.

8. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to inquiries, manage legitimate operational needs, comply with applicable obligations, resolve disputes, and maintain appropriate internal records. Retention periods may vary depending on the nature of the communication or service-related context.

9. Policy Changes

Sana Academy may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect operational, legal, or technical changes. If material revisions are made, the updated version will appear on this page with a revised last-updated date.

10. International Use

Because Sana Academy works with learners and families across multiple locations, information may be processed in jurisdictions different from your own. By using the site or contacting the academy, you understand that information may be handled across these operational contexts.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to make a privacy-related request, contact Sana Academy at hello@sana.edu or by phone at +1 (555) 123-4567.
